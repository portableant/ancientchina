Cai Lun presents the emperor with a new improved kind of paper.

Paper was first invented about two hundred years before. However, Cai Lun perfected the technique of making it. He presented this improved version to the emperor in A.D. 105. The first paper was made from pulped silk. Later the pulps of bamboo, hemp and wood were used. Paper was cheap to make, easy to write on and portable. Paper helped spread the growth of literacy in China as books became easier and cheaper to make.
