221 B.C. Qin Shi Huangdi becomes Emperor

After over 250 years of war Qin Shi Huangdi united China into an empire. He not only united the land, but also brought uniformity to it. Qin Shi Huangdi introduced one system for money, writing, weights and measures. He also joined up some of the defensive walls built during the Warring States period to form the Great Wall of China.
